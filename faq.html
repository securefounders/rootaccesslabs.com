Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about RootAccess Labs, our programs, and what to expect.

General

What is RootAccess Labs? +

RootAccess Labs (RAL) is a cybersecurity training and enablement platform focused on building real-world security capability. The training emphasizes attacker thinking, realistic scenarios, and practical judgment over theory or certifications.

How is RootAccess Labs different from other training institutes? +

Most programs focus on tools, certifications, or predefined labs.

RootAccess Labs focuses on:

  • How attacks actually unfold
  • How systems fail in practice
  • How security decisions are made under uncertainty

The emphasis is on thinking and execution, not memorization.

Is RootAccess Labs an institute or coaching center? +

No.

RootAccess Labs operates as a selective training and enablement platform, not a mass-market institute. Cohorts are intentionally small to maintain depth and quality.

Programs & Learning

What programs do you offer? +

RootAccess Labs currently offers:

  • SOC & Blue Team Operations (2 months)
  • Ethical Hacking (3 months)
  • Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing – VAPT (3 months)
  • Combined Track: Ethical Hacking + VAPT (4 months)
Are the programs beginner-friendly? +

Some programs are suitable for beginners, but all programs require seriousness and effort.

Advanced tracks assume prior foundational knowledge. Applicants are evaluated for readiness during the application process.

Is the curriculum day-wise or module-wise? +

The curriculum is module-wise.

Modules reflect learning progression and real-world workflows. Day-wise schedules exist only internally for instructors.

Is the training hands-on? +

Yes.

All programs are instructor-led and lab-heavy. Concepts are reinforced through realistic scenarios, exercises, and guided practice.

Is the training live or recorded? +

Training is primarily live and instructor-led.

Recordings, if provided, are supplementary and not a replacement for live participation.

What schedule do classes follow? +

Schedules vary by program and cohort. Typically, sessions are held on weekends or weekday evenings to accommodate working professionals.

Specific timings are shared during the application and onboarding process.

Admissions & Application

Why do I need to apply? +

Admission is selective.

The application helps ensure the program is a good fit — for you and for RootAccess Labs.

This is not an enrollment form.

What do you look for in an application? +

We evaluate:

  • Intent and seriousness
  • Readiness for hands-on learning
  • Willingness to put in effort
  • Alignment with program expectations

Background matters less than commitment and mindset.

Do you reject applications? +

Yes.

Not all applicants are accepted. Some may be deferred or advised to prepare further before reapplying.

When do new cohorts start? +

Cohort start dates are announced based on application volume and program readiness. Accepted applicants are notified in advance with sufficient time to prepare.

Outcomes & Expectations

Do you provide certificates? +

Certificates may be provided for completion, but they are not the focus.

Capability and understanding matter more than credentials.

Do you guarantee placements or jobs? +

No.

RootAccess Labs does not offer guaranteed placements, referrals, or job assurances.

The focus is skill and capability development.

How many hours per week are required? +

This varies by program, but all programs require:

  • Regular attendance
  • Hands-on practice outside sessions
  • Independent effort

Learners unwilling to commit time outside sessions may struggle.

Is this suitable if I already work full-time? +

Yes, for motivated learners — but it is demanding.

Time management and consistent effort are essential.

Tools, Certifications & Standards

Is the training aligned with international standards? +

The curriculum reflects globally accepted security practices and real-world workflows.

It is not designed around certification exams or compliance checklists.

Will I learn specific tools? +

You will use tools as part of learning, but training is tool-agnostic by design.

The emphasis is on understanding concepts that transfer across tools and environments.

Does this prepare me for certifications like CEH, OSCP, or Security+? +

While the training covers concepts that overlap with major certifications, the curriculum is not exam-focused.

The goal is to build foundational understanding that makes certifications easier to pursue independently if desired.

Policies & Miscellaneous

Do you offer refunds? +

Refund and cancellation policies are shared during onboarding and depend on the program stage. Please review them carefully before committing.

How can I contact you? +

You can reach out through the Contact or Apply page.

All serious inquiries are reviewed and responded to.

Do you offer corporate training? +

Corporate and organizational training may be available on a case-by-case basis. Please reach out through our contact page with details about your requirements.

Final Note

If you are looking for shortcuts, guarantees, or low-effort programs, RootAccess Labs may not be the right fit.

If you are serious about understanding how security actually works, you are welcome to apply.
